About Us

The “E-Education Website” (EEW) is for those customers how have lack of knowledge about the internet & if they want to learn something from internet. A Typical ‘Sites’ for a normal consumer searching makes it difficult & complex for what they want to search or learn because searching for a appropriate site for long hours will make the whole process time consume & no one has a time to waste it on internet in today’s scenario for the busy & fast life. The person who is Technically ‘illiterate’ will find this side a much handy way for searching.

First, the happy news: About Us usability has increased. The average success rate was 70% in Study 1 and 79% in Study 2.

The “E-Education Website” should play following key roles:

a. Less Time Consuming – EEW works on link concept so all other useful contents will be linked with this site in a defined category so instead of searching for hours & spending a much time it will be feasible to use.

b. Simplicity – All though practically there are many other sites on internet which work on same concept but those site are complex to use & hard to understand, due to our simplified coding user will enjoy using this site .

The Key Features of EEW are listed below:

• One Click Operation – This is a feature provide more flexibility to the use because instead for searching of different site for different learning user has to just click to our site & all the basic important site will be accessible for him/her. • Upgrades – This site will be upgraded time to time as per the new materials • Globalized Information – EEW is not only be consisting of study material of students but also other topics which are different from studies like cooking or Guitar learning will also be lead by this site, just a one click of you desired want & it will take you to that site.
